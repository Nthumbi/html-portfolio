About Me

Hi, I’m George — a passionate and curious full stack web developer with a growing toolkit and a big vision for the future of the web.

My journey into web development started with a simple fascination: how does the internet work? That question led me down a rabbit hole of HTML tags, JavaScript functions, CSS rules, and eventually, full stack application development. Today, I specialize in building dynamic, user-focused websites and applications using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Git. I enjoy crafting clean, responsive interfaces on the front end and building solid, efficient logic on the back end.

What drives me is the thrill of problem-solving and the opportunity to bring ideas to life in the browser. Whether I’m debugging a stubborn piece of code or pushing a new feature to GitHub, I’m constantly learning and growing — because in tech, standing still isn’t an option.

Looking ahead, I aspire to contribute to projects that make a real impact, from startups to open-source communities. I’m especially interested in building tools that empower others, improve accessibility, and make the web a more beautiful and inclusive place.

Thanks for stopping by — feel free to check out my projects or get in touch!
